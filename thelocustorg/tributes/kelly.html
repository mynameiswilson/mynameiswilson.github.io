Kelly



i think that this picture is great. within its tiny confines and suspect resolution, it captures all that is kelly and i. i adore that smiling face every day of my life, and i couldn't be happier.

kelly is ...
my friend,
my partner,
my compatriot,
my coconspirator,
my rival,
my onus,
my gift,
my tiger,
my dragon,
my weeping willow,
my sturdy pine,
my ugly duckling,
my raven-haired goddess,
my butcher,
my baker,
my comfy afghan maker,
my one love,
my true love,
my everything.

kelly bear, i love you. happy valentines day!

2002.02.14
