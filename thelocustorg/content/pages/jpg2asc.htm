notes:
  • you must put what jpg2ascii gives you in a <PRE> tag, else the lines will break, and sadness will cross the land.
  • this works wonderfully in Firebird for Windows. IE for Windows, and Firebird on Linux don't do so well. I suspect it's the font-rendering undercarriage. Firebird on Linux using GTK2 and XFT (anti-aliased fonts) works a little better than just the stock GTK1.2 non-XFT build, but so far the raining champ is Firebird for Win32.
  • you can scale the fonts on a page by doing CTRL+- and CTRL+SHFT+=, or by holding CTRL and using your mousewheel.
