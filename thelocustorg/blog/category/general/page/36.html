Apr22

a question of physics, and squirrels.

“For Janet, her help came from her faith, but it also came from a squirrel…”

Kelly and I have installed a bird-feeder out front of our yard. We have a pair of water-maples out there with some branches are fairly odd angles (parallel to the ground for a good distance). These branches are good for hanging things on, you see. Things such as bird feeders. Now, we filled up the bird feeder on Saturday, and our bird feeder has a capacity of up to 4 pounds of food (no word on actual *volume*). Today, as of this morning, the food was approximately two-thirds gone. I don’t care how many birds you have eating, that’s a lot of food for finches. Perhaps this is the work of a SQUIRREL? My hypothesis is no. Others say yes. In any case, I need proof. VISUAL PROOF. More later.

Apr14

Houston – Day Four

Man oh man, I felt sick yesterday. After we walked home from the The Flying Saucer, I just felt very tired. Not necessarily drunk, as I had only had two beers whilst throwing darts. Ugh. Went to sleep queasy and didn’t sleep well. My legs and back hurt, and just ugh… Anyway, we did manage to make it out to the Houston Museum of Natural Science, which was great! Plenty of big ol’ dinosaur skeletons, lots and lots (and lots) of gems and precious stones, a great couple of exhibits on indigenous American cultures, etc. Fun!

We finally did make it out to World Market, the Pier One-like store without all the crap. (And it’s cheaper, too). So, that was excruciatingly nice. (I was still illin’). We retired to home for a bit, and then headed out to grab something to eat at Fuddruckers. Still not feeling 100%, I ordered a grilled-chicken big bowl salad, and Kelly had the veggie burger. It took them 10 minutes to cook up the damned veggie burger! Oh well, we *are* in Texas. My salad was good, but they put bacon on it. Well, I shouldn’t say *on* it, because the bacon was infused into this salad. Well, the chicken was good.

Oh, that’s really all of action-packed day 4 of our journey. We retired home and watched some stuff on Matt’s Tivo. (Note to self: get Tivo).

I suggest the Houston – Day Four Gallery and nice pinot grigio.
